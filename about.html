About Me

Hi, I'm Jon Magnusson, a music producer and composer based in Stockholm, Sweden. My journey in music spans various genres including classical, jazz, indie folk, and rock.

Despite not having a traditional degree, I have honed my craft through self-learning and collaborations, continuously pushing creative boundaries.

My vision for music is a never-ending journey with an unknown destination, bringing emotion and depth to every piece I create.
